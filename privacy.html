Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

B2OPS ("we," "our," or "us") provides a retail operations and customer loyalty management platform for independent retail businesses. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information through the B2OPS platform and its associated services.

By using B2OPS, business operators and their customers agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Business account information — store name, owner name, email address, and phone number provided during account registration.
  • Customer data — names, phone numbers, email addresses, birthdays, and loyalty activity entered by business operators on behalf of their customers.
  • Transaction data — sales records, product information, inventory levels, discount activity, and coupon usage logged through the platform.
  • Team member data — names and email addresses of staff accounts created by business owners.
  • SMS consent records — opt-in status, opt-in timestamps, and opt-out records for customer SMS communications.
  • Device and session data — browser type, device identifiers, and session activity used to maintain account security.
  • Third-party service credentials — Twilio API credentials entered by business operators for SMS functionality, stored securely and never shared.

How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the B2OPS platform.
  • To enable business operators to manage their customers, sales, inventory, and loyalty programs.
  • To facilitate SMS communications between businesses and their opted-in customers.
  • To send platform-related notifications, feature announcements, and maintenance alerts to business operators.
  • To detect and prevent unauthorized access or fraudulent activity.

SMS Communications

B2OPS facilitates SMS messaging between retail businesses and their customers through Twilio. Each business operates its own Twilio account and is responsible for obtaining proper customer consent before sending messages.

Customer phone numbers are collected only with explicit consent through a double opt-in process. Customers may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Full details are available in our SMS Consent Policy.

B2OPS does not use customer phone numbers for any purpose other than facilitating communications requested by the business operator.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections, access controls, and session management to protect your information.

Third-party API credentials (such as Twilio Account SID and Auth Token) are stored in encrypted form and are only accessible by the business account that provided them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers necessary to operate the platform (such as Supabase for database services and Twilio for SMS delivery).
  • When required by law or in response to valid legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of B2OPS, its operators, or the public.

Data Retention

Business account data is retained for as long as the account is active. Upon account termination or deletion, data is removed within 30 days. SMS opt-out records are retained indefinitely to ensure compliance with opt-out requests.

Your Rights

Business operators may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their account data at any time by contacting us. End customers wishing to have their data removed should contact the retail business they enrolled with directly.

Children's Privacy

B2OPS is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Business operators will be notified of material changes through the platform. Continued use of B2OPS after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
